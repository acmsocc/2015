Amenities and concessions

Note: you must book your room through the SoCC registration site to receive these benefits.

Internet

Free in-room HSIA (High Speed Internet Access).

Parking

$5 per night for self-parking offered to SoCC attendees staying at the Hilton. The normal rate is $25.

Optional resort fee

The resort fee is waived for SoCC attendees. Normally, it is a mandatory $25 per day. However, attendees may purchase a Daily Resort Package at the reduced rate of $15 per room, per day to get the following benefits. PlayStation 3 access with unlimited movies and games; no access fee for local, toll-free and credit card calls; access to Hawaiian Cultural Activities; 20% discount on Lagoon Beach toys; 10% discount on Ocean Sports cruises.

Fitness room

SoCC attendees have complementary access to the Fitness Center between 5AM-11PM with their room key. This includes the cardio room and strength training room. It does not include access to the Kohala Spa or use of Jacuzzi, steam room, lockers, or showers (available for $20 fee).

Day care

Club Keiki offers full day, half day and night camp programs for children ages 5-12. Reservations are required. Please call (808) 886-1234 extension 1202 by 4:00 pm the day prior to make your reservation. Click here for website, brochure and prices: clubkeikiwaikoloa.com/camp.

Malahini Keiki Care offers babysitting and nanny service for children of all ages. Call (808) 331-2909 for reservations, or visit website for more info: mkcare.com
